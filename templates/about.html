About Academic Plagiarism Checker

Welcome to the Academic Plagiarism Checker, a tool designed to ensure the integrity of academic work by detecting plagiarism in documents. Our app is equipped to handle various file formats, including PDF and ZIP files.

Key Features

  • Supports checking plagiarism for AI-generated text
  • Accepts documents in PDF and ZIP formats
  • Provides detailed results with the option to download in CSV format
  • Fast and reliable plagiarism detection algorithm

AI-Generated Text

Our plagiarism checker is specifically designed to handle content generated by AI systems. We understand the unique challenges posed by AI-generated text and have implemented advanced algorithms to accurately identify and flag instances of potential plagiarism.

How It Works

Simply upload your document in PDF or ZIP format, and our tool will analyze the content, comparing it against a vast database to identify potential instances of plagiarism. The results will be presented in a clear and concise format, allowing you to take appropriate actions to maintain academic integrity.

If you have any questions, concerns, or feedback, feel free to contact us. We value your input and are dedicated to continuously improving our plagiarism checker to meet your needs.
